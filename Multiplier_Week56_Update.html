JUNE EXECUTION

Priority Action Status
P0 Xindus Air+Sea freight — targeting >70% of inward volume in June
Xindus ₹334/kg vs UPS Air ₹590/kg (June rate). May was 43% via Xindus, saving ~$96K vs 100% Air. >70% Xindus in June saves ~$75K/month vs 100% UPS Air baseline — more than May because UPS is now ₹590 (was ₹516). Full conversion: ~$107K/month.		 Executing

NPD team (Mrunmayee / Sanjib) has proposed switching gummies pouches from vertical to horizontal placement, increasing units per shipper from 18 → 24 (~30% better loadability). Shipper dimension trials completed at Advama. Results under review. Estimated saving: ~$8,246/month.		 In Progress
